About Us

Science-based vaccine education for healthier communities.

Why We Exist

Vaccine hesitancy has been identified by the World Health Organization as one of the top ten threats to global health. Misinformation spreads faster than any virus, and the consequences are measured in preventable illness, hospitalization, and death. Veba Vaccinates exists to counter misinformation with clarity, compassion, and rigorous science — meeting people where they are with respect for their concerns while providing the factual foundation they need to make informed decisions.
